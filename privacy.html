Privacy Policy

At Winpact, we take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our browser extension.

1. Introduction

Winpact is designed to be privacy-first. We believe that doing good shouldn't come at the cost of your personal information.

2. What data do we collect?

We collect only the minimum necessary data to provide our service:

  • An anonymous user ID (UUID) to track your impact without identifying you personally.
  • Information about the stores where you have activated our extension to process donations.
  • The number of activations to calculate your impact score.

3. How do we use your data?

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Ensure the functionality of our extension.
  • Calculate and display your impact score.
  • Create statistics for our donation activities.

4. Data sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. We do not sell your data. All data is processed securely and used only for the purposes stated above.

5. Your rights

You have the right to request information about your stored data at any time and to have it deleted. Since we only store anonymous IDs, we may need your specific ID from the extension settings to fulfill such requests.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through our website.
