Story

Winpact started as a student project at RWTH Aachen University. The idea was simple: Why can't we automatically do good while shopping online, without spending extra money? We developed a technology that converts marketing budgets from major online shops into donations for nature, humans, and animals. We are not a faceless corporation, but a team of students using technology to solve real-world problems.
